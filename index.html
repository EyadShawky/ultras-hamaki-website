Who is Ultras Hamaki ?

"Ultras Hamaki" is a group of youth who are known for their loyality and their endless, effective support for The MegaStar Mohamed Hamaki. Founded on 13th February 2011 Recognized as the First and most active "Ultras" for a singer in the middle east

ABOUT HAMAKI

MOHAMED HAMAKI

An Egyptian artist.

Hamaki (Mohamed Ibrahim Mohamed El Hamaki), an Egyptian Super Star of his own kind emerged to life on the 4th of November 1975, Cairo Egypt.

Hamaki the child had a great ability to listen to any type of music and play it on a number of instruments especially the key board.

Hamaki’s domestic life is just as pleasing as his musical career. He cherishes and dedicates his time with his family.
