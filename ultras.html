Who is Ultras Hamaki ?

"Ultras Hamaki" is a group of youth who are known for their loyality and their endless, effective support for The MegaStar Mohamed Hamaki. Founded on 13th February 2011 Recognized as the First and most active "Ultras" for a singer in the middle east

What makes us different from any other Fan Group ?

Our unique ways of supporting Hamaki set us apart. For instance, our constant presence in nearly all Hamaki's events. In addition to displaying banners, warming up the audience, creating a encouraging atmosphere, beside designing, and celebrating Hamaki's birthday yearly and much more. But out of all, the most important is providing Group Vocal Support in some songs
